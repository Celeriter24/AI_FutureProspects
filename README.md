# AI FutureProspects
My personal (tried to be as realistic and rational) thoughts about the future of the world with two different AI Scenarios after the impressive [Anthropic report](https://www.anthropic.com/institute/recursive-self-improvement) "When AI builds itself" came out two days ago. 

The original was written in German. 
You will find one translation into English by Goggle Gemma 4.

I just had to share my opinion.

### Feel free to join the discussion or submit issues/pull requests to improve this Resource.

I’m interested in your opinions.

### Summary: 

The rapid advancement of Artificial Intelligence presents a paradigm shift comparable in scale to the Industrial Revolution. This post argues that the current rate of technological advancement significantly outpaces the necessary overhaul of our socio-economic, regulatory, and educational infrastructure. Strategic preparation is mandatory.
